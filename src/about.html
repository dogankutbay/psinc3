About Us

About Our Company

Paramount Solutions & Global Services, Inc. (PSI) is a certified small minority and disadvantaged woman-owned business founded in August 2004 as an engineering consultancy that has rapidly evolved into a quality-oriented organization with a strong imprint for providing Systems Engineering, Information Assurance, and Training services as our core competencies.

Since our inception, we have expanded our services to provide a full complement of staffing solutions across several expertise such as Project/Program Managers, Healthcare and Medical Administrative, Records Management, Paralegal/Legal Assistants, and Contracts Administrators and Managers.

Over the years, our clients has experienced an outstanding team that offers first-rate project management execution with the comprehensive knowledge and expertise to undertake projects tasks that matches our capabilities. Our experience has taught us to identify great talent and provide our customers with the right candidates the first time.

Our Guiding Principles

Responsive - Have you ever sent an email or left a voice message and waited days or weeks for a response? Not with us! You can expect a quick response from our team and if you don't receive one, we want to hear from you.

Reliable - Promises, promises, promises... At PSI, we do what we say and say what we mean. You can be assured to receive straight answers from us. No fluff... if we cannot do something, we will let you know and offer a referral to you. We are not experts at everything!

Resourceful - Doing more with less has become today's practice and why not? Technology has advanced and our workforce has excelled and stepped up to the challenge. Our clients can expect employees with the precision in the disciplines they require. You can expect to get what you need. We aim to get it right the first time!
